WHO WE ARE :

We are an association of women with a shared interest in cultural, social and international activities and provide an attractive cultural and social programme.

We organise outings to the theatre, galleries and places of interest.

The Edinburgh Club is a member of the International Association of Lyceum Clubs and members are invited to attend the IALC Congress and Cultural Visits.

We meet for lunch at the Royal Scots Club and this is followed by a talk given by an invited speaker.
We meet at 12:00 for a 12:30 lunch and the meeting ends at around 3pm.

We welcome new members, so do come along and enjoy a delicious two course lunch and a coffee with friendly company - followed by an interesting talk!
