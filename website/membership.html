OUR MEMBERSHIP

Membership fees are £25 per year to be paid by 1 October.

Monthly meetings commence on the third Saturday of October and continue through to the third Saturday of April.

From May through to September members can meet on the third Saturday of the month for lunch at the Royal Scots Club.

We do not have club meetings over the summer however we have a summer programme of visits to theatres, galleries and places of interest.

The syllabus is issued in September with subscriptions to be paid by 1 October.

Records show that there was an Edinburgh Lyceum Club from 1938. There is a long history, the members continue to support the club, to keep the programme interesting and to continue the club's longevity.
