CLUB MEETING
Join us at our next club meeting on the 18th of February 2023 at the Royal Scots Club at 12:00!

After a lovely lunch we will have a guest speaker "Clea Friend" - a professional cellist and community musician.

Clea will be presenting "A Musician's Life: A New Model".

If you are interested in coming along - please contact us before the event so that a seat could be reserved for you.
